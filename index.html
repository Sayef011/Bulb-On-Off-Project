Blub

LED lightbulbs are very energy-efficient compared with incandescent lightbulbs. An incandescent lightbulb emits almost as much heat as light: 95% of the energy used by an incandescent lightbulb turns into heat, while only 5% becomes visible light. By comparison, an LED bulb converts around 80% of the energy into light.

Table Fan

Table fans consume less electricity, and some models even come with energy-saving modes. If you have limited floor space, you can go for wall mounted fans. Such fans also deliver a strong airflow, and you can also use them for ventilating rooms. They are easy to install and can also be removed when needed.
